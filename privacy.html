Stickly - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Stickly App ("we," "our," or "us") collects minimal information to provide our service:

  • Local Data: Your todos and theme preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Subscription Data: Purchase information through Apple and RevenueCat for subscription management
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for subscription validation
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve our service

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Stickly app functionality
  • To process subscription payments through Apple
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information except:

  • Apple: Payment processing and App Store functionality
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and analytics
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

4. Data Storage

  • Your todos are stored locally on your device only
  • Subscription data is managed by Apple and RevenueCat
  • We do not have access to your personal todo content

5. Your Rights

  • Access your subscription information
  • Cancel your subscription anytime in iOS Settings
  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us for data-related questions

6. Children's Privacy

Stickly is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
